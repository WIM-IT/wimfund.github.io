About Us

WIM Asset Management was founded in April, 2008. The team, specialised in global equity long/short, is formed by a stable group of experienced professionals with proven strategy to capture alpha. The ultimate goal is to sustain and grow wealth for our clients.

WIM Global Fund was launched in Jan, 2010. It has a Greater China-centric equity long/short strategy adopting a top-down and bottom-up approach in constructing the portfolio while following SRI and ESG practice. The fund aims 15%+ annualized return by seeking alpha generation and managing beta risks through in-depth research and dynamic global equity allacation.
